About Me

I am a graduate student in mathematics at MIT. My advisors are Tomasz Mrowka and Clifford Taubes (Harvard University). I am supported by a National Science Foundation Graduate Research Fellowship.

My research interests are in global analysis and differential geometry, specifically on the analytic and geometric aspects of mathematical gauge theory. I am currently studying studying generalized Seiberg-Witten equations and Z2-harmonic spinors.
